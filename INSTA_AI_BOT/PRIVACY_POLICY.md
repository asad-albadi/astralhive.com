# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04.04.2026

Astral Hive ("we", "our", or "us") operates the AI assistant "Astral" via Instagram.

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you interact with Astral.

## 1. Information We Collect

We do not actively collect or store personal data outside of what is necessary to provide our service.

However, the following may be processed during interactions:

- Messages you send to Astral via Instagram
- Information you voluntarily provide (e.g., name, phone number, delivery details for orders)

We do not store chat logs or user data outside of the Instagram platform unless required to fulfill a request (such as processing an order).

## 2. Platform Data (Instagram)

Astral operates on Instagram. Any data you provide is also subject to :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}’s privacy policies and data handling practices.

We do not control how Instagram collects, stores, or processes your data.

## 3. AI Processing

Messages may be processed by:
- External AI providers (such as :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}), or
- Locally hosted AI models

This processing is used solely to generate responses and provide assistance.

We do not use your data for profiling, selling, or advertising.

## 4. Orders and Voluntary Information

If you choose to place an order, you may be asked to provide:
- Name
- Phone number
- Delivery details

This information is used only to fulfill your request and is not stored beyond operational necessity.

## 5. Data Retention

- We do not maintain independent databases of user conversations.
- Any temporary data is handled only as needed to provide the service.

## 6. Your Rights

You may:
- Stop interacting with Astral at any time
- Request to opt out of future interactions
- Request deletion of any information you have voluntarily provided

## 7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information during processing. However, no system is completely secure.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

Astral is not intended for children under the age of 13.
